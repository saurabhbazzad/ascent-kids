CURRICULUM

The unique Ascent Junior Camps English Program has been developed by international educational experts to help children gain language skills from the earliest possible stage, and use English in the real world with confidence.

The curriculum includes four distinct levels. Across all levels, five key learning skills have been identified and are practiced at the same time to boost the learning process and help children to feel at home with the English language: speaking, listening and understanding.

Each unit of the Ascent Junior Camps English Program is based on nurturing your child’s imagination and helping them learn in a way that ensures the knowledge will last a lifetime.

Our experts do this by combining fun, playful education techniques with enough structure and routine to manage their knowledge-base and prepare them for full-time education.

We provide children with activities, multimedia and sensory experiences that enhance the learning process, as well as a relaxed, open environment in which to explore their new talent.

CERTIFICATION

Your child will:

  • gain confidence in expressing themselves in English and expand their communicative abilities
  • deal with topics of interest to their age group in class
  • expand their grammar and vocabulary, and learn to use the language in a natural way
  • develop as a person ready for a changing, globalized environment.
Apart from this, a formal certification of English Language Achievement will be awarded.
Upon completion of your chosen Program with us, your child will be given a Certificate of Achievement, which will help to fill them with confidence and reinforce what they’ve learned, as well as indicating the skills learned to future education providers

The Child Student Program Ascent Kids is specially designed to improve the speaking and listening skills of children aged 6-8. Children learn through our intensive listening and video tutorials, where our experienced trainers would be assisting for improving vocabulary, pronunciation and encouraging students to speak immensely during the training program.
As most of these lessons are vocabulary-based, using videos, students entertainment based learning activities is initiated and implemented, for better improvement of English language.

PROGRAM STRUCTURE

Speaking Competency

  1. Basic Introduction
    1. Greetings
    2. Express feelings in words
    3. Identifying Social Activity Spaces
  2. Enriching Kids Vocabulary
    1. Point out parts of body
    2. Stating the relationship of family members
    3. Recognizing objects & Colors, Singular Plural
    4. Understanding Days of Week, Months
    5. Understanding Time
    6. Birds, Vegetables, Fruits, Vehicles
  3. Self-Introduction
    1. Talking about one-self
    2. Introduction to action words
    3. Talking about a picture
  4. Communicative Grammar
    1. Vowel & Consonant
    2. The verb ‘to be’
    3. Basic of tense
    4. Speaking! The Present Continuous Tense
  5. Phonics Development -I
    1. Introduction to Consonant Diagraph & Blend
    2. ‘Fl’, ‘bl’, ‘sl’, ‘cl’, ‘pl’, ‘bl’, ‘gr’, ‘tr’, ‘br’, ‘cr’, ‘dr’ sounds
    3. ‘pr’, ‘fr’, ‘wh’, ‘st’, ‘sw’, ‘sp’, ‘sc’, ‘sn’, ‘sm’, ‘sk’ sounds
    4. ‘st’, ‘sh’, ‘ch’, ‘th’, ‘wh’, ‘ph’ ‘ng’ Sounds
  6. Phonics Development -II
    1. Introduction to Diphthong
    2. “ia”, “io”, “ou”, “aw”/“au”, “oy”/“oi”, “ow”/“ou”
    3. “oo”, “oa”, “ea”, “ai”
    4. “ur”, “ew”
  7. Live Project on Listening/Speaking Skills
